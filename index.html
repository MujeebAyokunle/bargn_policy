Bargn Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 3, 2024

At Bargn, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your personal information to ensure transparency and security while you use our app to find the best deals in your location.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address, and password, which are collected for authentication purposes only.
  • Location Data: Used to identify and display deals near your location.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for the following purposes:

  1. Authentication: To verify your identity and grant secure access to the app.
  2. Location-Based Services: To show relevant deals and discounts in your vicinity.
  3. App Improvement: To enhance our features and optimize the user experience based on anonymous usage patterns.

Data Sharing Policy

Bargn does not share, sell, or distribute your personal data to third parties. Your data is used only internally and in compliance with this Privacy Policy.

Payment Security

All payments made through Bargn are processed via secure and trusted payment gateways. We do not store your payment details on our servers. To ensure your safety:

  • Always use secure devices when accessing Bargn.
  • Protect your authentication credentials.

User Responsibilities

To maintain account security and enjoy seamless access to our services, users are advised to:

  • Keep login credentials confidential.
  • Notify us immediately in case of unauthorized access to their accounts.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Once your account is deleted, all associated personal data will be permanently removed from our systems.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our services or legal requirements. Please review this policy periodically to stay informed.

This Privacy Policy ensures that your personal data is handled responsibly and transparently while you enjoy exclusive deals through Bargn.
